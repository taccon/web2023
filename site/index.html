Over the years we created many software projects. Some of them still exist and you can find them here on our homepage. Others were discontinued. But we love all of them!

Cetup is one of our oldest projects. It started its life in 2002 as "InstallChild" which was developed as a free alternative for commercial installers for Windows. It made it very easy to create your own installations by following a simple assistant interface. The project was renamed to Cetup later and large parts were rewritten. The first version of Cetup still worked with an assistant interface but also featured a custom script engine called "Ckript" allowed you to change almost everything about the installation process.

After not being maintained for a couple of years, Cetup has been rewritten from the scratch and came back as a software that allows you to easily create installations based on NSIS, still combining the simplicity of an assistant interface with the scripting power of NSIS, if it's needed.

More information and a download link can be found on the dedicated Cetup website!

Bringing the fun back to listening to your MP3 collection. Or FLAC collection. Or OGG collection. Or anything-you-like collection.

Is 2022 still time for a classic media player like back in the old days? Sure it is! While most of the time you enjoy streaming services, there is still a huge collection of high-quality files somewhere on your hard-drive waiting for you.

New audioplayer3 lets you discover them again and organise them in playlists. No bloated media library but simple management that does not destroy your folder structure.
